Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find Tactik in my phone when sharing a link?

Once you select share, scroll to more on your device and scroll again to select Tactik.

How do I use Tactik?

If you tap on the question mark icon in the top right hand corner on the collections screen, you can scroll left and right to view our in app tutorial.

How do I share a saved link?

Go to the collection and board that the link is saved in and swipe the link to the left. From here you can edit or share your link.

How do I rename a link?

When saving a link to Tactik, you can press the X in the top right corner and type in the name you prefer. You can also add tags by tapping on the + sign below the link.

How do I create a Board?

Whether already saving a link or not, you can choose your collection and then tap on the plus sign to create a new board and select your icon.

How do I edit or delete a collection and board?

When on the collection or board page swipe the collection or board to the left and edit or delete from there.
